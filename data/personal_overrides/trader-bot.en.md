An algorithmic trading bot for Interactive Brokers, built around `ib-async` and a clean six-interface strategy pattern (universe filter, signal generator, position sizer, portfolio allocator, execution algo, exit logic) assembled by a composer so a new strategy only has to implement those roles. The one strategy currently wired in trades an EMA9/EMA21 bullish crossover confirmed by RSI and MACD histogram, sizes each position as a fixed fraction of strategy capital, skips symbols going ex-dividend within a configurable window, executes via slightly aggressive limit orders, and exits either on a bearish crossover or on a drawdown threshold from the position's session peak. Around that core sit a circuit breaker and risk manager, an APScheduler-driven job for separate EU and US session windows, Telegram notifications, Prometheus metrics and a health-check endpoint, and PostgreSQL persistence through SQLAlchemy and Alembic migrations.

The project reads as a genuinely developed piece of trading infrastructure rather than a toy script, with a real dependency-injected architecture and a dev-only backtesting dependency (vectorbt) kept out of the production container. No credentials or account data were found in the codebase; the description above covers only the strategy and system architecture, not any live trading results, which are not present in the repository.
