# IT project management platform with Gantt charts

**Sector**: language services and professional translation company

**Period**: to be confirmed

**Role**: IT Manager

**Technologies**: OpenProject (self-hosted), Gantt charts

## Context

Planning for the IT department's activities had no dedicated tool for visualizing roadmaps and
dependencies between tasks, making it difficult to get an overall view of ongoing projects and
their deadlines.

## What was done

Deployment and maintenance of OpenProject, an open source project management platform, self-hosted
on the internal network, to organize the IT department's activities through Gantt charts.

## Result

An overall view of planned IT activities and their time dependencies, with an internally maintained
open source tool instead of a third-party SaaS service.
