# Time and attendance software migration

**Sector**: language services and professional translation company

**Period**: to be confirmed

**Role**: IT Manager, systems administrator

**Technologies**: Windows Server, time and attendance management software

## Context

The company's staff time and attendance tracking software needed to be migrated to a more solid
infrastructure, so it would remain reliable over time without a dedicated technical contact
beyond the IT Manager.

## What was done

Migration of the company's time and attendance tracking software to a dedicated Windows server,
including its configuration and rollout to production for the entire staff.

## Result

Reliable time and attendance tracking for all company staff, with no significant service
disruption during or after the migration.
